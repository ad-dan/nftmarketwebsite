Smart Contracts

Open-source, audited contracts that power the marketplace.

NFTCollection.sol

ERC-721 Collection

Mint NFTs with metadata stored on IPFS. Supports minting, token URI resolution, and collection-level management.

NFTAuction.sol

Auction Engine

Create timed auctions, place bids, settle sales, and distribute fees. Supports starting price, reserve, and fee splitting.

ContractRegistry.sol

Registry & Discovery

Registers all NFT collections on-chain. Enables discovery of every collection deployed through the marketplace.
